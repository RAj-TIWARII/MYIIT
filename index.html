Career opportunities in the IT sector

Elevate your skills with our most in-demand programmes

Career opportunities in the IT sector can be broadly classified into the two main fields of hardware and software. Under hardware, you have manufacturing, maintenance, R &D and management. You have manufacturing, development, programming, testing, maintenance, and support under software. The related areas are computer operations, database administration, and data centre management. The IT sector offers an assortment of career opportunities. A regular IT professional can start their career with the job responsibilities like database administrators, junior programmers, software developers, website developers, data analysts and data scientists trainees.

The highest paying IT jobs are:

Artificial intelligence professionals.
Cyber security professionals.
Machine learning professionals.
After graduating from a university, a fresher will typically undergo training for a certain period and then move ahead in the scalar chain as a software developer or project leader.
